About Critical Counter

If you don't know what a Critical Mass is, please refer to the tutorial.

Knowing how many people have been at your CriticalMass is a memory, cool for the stats nerds and also helps you to decide where to go. Also you are able to see live statistics about your current mass.

This App aims to automate everything but counting. Every time you start the counter, it checks your location and saves the number of bikes you counted into a database.

That allows you to see where and when you counted how many bikes.

In the future I might collaborate with Malte from criticalmass.in and give him access to the data.

Q: What kind of data do you store?

A: I want to store only the data neccessary for the service. So here is what is stored:

  • A bike was counted (acutally: Someone clicked on the app)
  • Date, time and location of that event
  • What phone counted the bike. Actually I generate one anonymous identifier which is used to not get your count confused with others. I don't know who you are, what device you use, or what you look like. And I really don't care. :)
